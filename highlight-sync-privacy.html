Privacy Policy — Highlight Sync

Last updated: 2026-04-25

What we collect

  • Anonymous user ID (generated on first run via Firebase Anonymous Auth)
  • Highlighted text, the URL and page title where it was made, your chosen color/style, optional comments, custom page labels, and folder names you create

How it is stored

All data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) under your user ID so that your highlights sync across your own devices. We do not sell or share your data with any third party.

What we do NOT collect

  • Your name, email, IP address, or any personally identifying information
  • Browsing history outside of pages where you explicitly create a highlight
  • Payment information

Deletion

You can delete any individual highlight or folder from the viewer. To delete all your data, contact us at the address below.
